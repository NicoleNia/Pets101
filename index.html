Why did we start Pets 101?

  1. Educate pet owners on the responsibility of their pet

    Numerous individuals adopt their friends without knowing the responsibilities behind it. Our goal is to help you through your journey with your new (or old) companion and maybe, show you the right pet for you.

  2. Protect pets nationwide

    Approximately 12.6 million companions have been admitted into shelters in the past 20 years. Pets 101 makes it easier to connect with organizations and get involved with the solution.
